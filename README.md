## 💡 Why Immo-Boussole?
Finding the ideal property is a real challenge: scattered listings, incomplete information, subjective opinions… **Immo-Boussole** is a collaborative app that helps you:
- **Centralize** your favorite listings (LeBonCoin, SeLoger, etc.)
- **Evaluate** each property as a team, with ratings and comments
- **Visualize** your "ideal profile" automatically generated from your top picks

More than a tool, it’s a **method** to make the right decision, together.
